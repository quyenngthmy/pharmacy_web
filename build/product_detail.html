What is ashwagandha?

Ashwagandha is a shrub with small yellow flowers that grows across parts of India, Africa, and the Middle East. The roots and leaves of this plant are turned into powders or extracts which is used inside supplements.

What are the potential benefits of ashwagandha?

Ashwagandha has been used as a home remedy and it's said to help with:

  • Stress relief
  • Energy
  • Sex drive
  • Cognitive performance

However, there is little clinical evidence to support these claims, and more research needs to be carried out before it can be recommended. Please bear in mind that you should consult a healthcare professional before taking ashwagandha supplements and to continue to take any prescribed medication.
